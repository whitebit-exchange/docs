Market statistics for the current UTC day, resetting daily at 00:00 UTC. **Update interval:** 1 second.

<WsChannelOverview operations={channelOperations} />

## Query market today (one-time)

`params`: `[market]`. Only one market per request.

<Note>
To get data for multiple markets, either make separate requests or use the subscription method.
</Note>

<WsMessageExample data={exMarketTodayRequest} />

## Subscribe to market today

<WsSubscribeSteps
  subscribe={exMarketTodaySubscribe}
  subscribeNote="Pass one or more market names in params."
  confirmation={{id: 1, result: {status: "success"}, error: null}}
  update={exMarketTodayUpdate}
  updateNote="params[0] is the market name. params[1] is the statistics object."
  unsubscribe={{id: 2, method: "marketToday_unsubscribe", params: []}}
/>

<Note>
All statistics reset to zero at UTC midnight (00:00 UTC) each day.
</Note>
