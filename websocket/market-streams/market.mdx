## Query market statistics (one-time)

Fetch market statistics for any period without subscribing.

`params`: `[market, period_in_seconds]`.

## Subscribe to market statistics

<Warning>
Subscriptions support only a **24-hour period** (86400 seconds). Custom period subscriptions are not supported.
</Warning>

<WsSubscribeSteps
  subscribe={exMarketSubscribe}
  subscribeNote="Pass one or more market names in params. Subscriptions always use a 24-hour (86400s) rolling window."
  confirmation={{id: 1, result: {status: "success"}, error: null}}
  update={exMarketUpdate}
  unsubscribe={{id: 2, method: "market_unsubscribe", params: []}}
/>
